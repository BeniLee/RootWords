單元二十三：【來】 -ven-

Part 1. 核心單字學習

convene (v.) 集會

The committee will convene next week.

委員會將於下週集會。

convention (n.) 會議；慣例

The convention attracted thousands of people.

這場會議吸引了數千人。

conventional (adj.) 傳統的

She has very conventional views.

她的觀點非常傳統。

intervene (v.) 介入

The teacher had to intervene in the fight.

老師必須介入這場爭鬥。

contravene (v.) 違反

This law contravenes human rights.

這項法律違反人權。

revenue (n.) 收入

The company's revenue increased this year.

公司今年的收入增加了。

avenue (n.) 大道

The parade went down the main avenue.

遊行沿著主要大道前進。

venue (n.) 地點

The wedding venue was beautiful.

婚禮場地很美。

tradition (n.) 傳統

It is a tradition to eat mooncakes.

「吃月餅是一項傳統。」

intervention (n.) 介入

The teacher's intervention stopped the fight.

「老師的介入阻止了爭鬥。」

conventional (adj.) 傳統的

She has very conventional views on marriage.

「她對婚姻的看法很傳統。」

contravention (n.) 違反

He was fined for contravention of the rules.

「他因違反規定而被罰款。」

revenue (n.) 收入

The government relies on tax revenue.

「政府依賴稅收。」

interfere (v.) 干涉

Don't interfere in other people's business.

「不要干涉別人的事。」
