Part 1. 核心單字學習

sonic (adj.) 音波的

The sonic boom was heard across the city.

音爆的響聲傳遍了整個城市。

supersonic (adj.) 超音速的

The supersonic jet broke the sound barrier.

超音速噴射機突破了音障。

ultrasonic (adj.) 超音波的

Doctors use ultrasonic waves to see the baby.

醫生用超音波來查看胎兒。

resonate (v.) 共鳴、迴響

His speech resonated with the audience.

他的演講引起了觀眾的共鳴。

dissonance (n.) 不和諧

The dissonance in the music was intentional.

音樂中的不和諧是故意的。

consonant (n.) 子音

The English alphabet has 21 consonants.

英文字母有 21 個子音。

discord (n.) 不和

There was discord among the team members.

團隊成員之間有分歧不和。

concord (n.) 和諧

The two nations signed a concord of peace.

兩國簽署了和平協定。

cordial (adj.) 真誠的、熱情的

They gave us a cordial welcome.

他們給了我們真誠的歡迎。

chord (n.) 和弦

He played a beautiful chord on the piano.

他在鋼琴上彈了一個美麗的和弦。
