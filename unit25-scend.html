Part 1. 核心單字學習

ascend (v.) 上升

The balloon will ascend into the sky.

氣球將升上天空。

ascent (n.) 攀登

The ascent of the mountain was difficult.

攀登這座山很困難。

descend (v.) 下降

The plane began to descend for landing.

飛機開始下降準備著陸。

descendant (n.) 後代

He is a descendant of the king.

他是國王的後代。

condescend (v.) 屈尊

The boss condescended to speak to us.

老闆屈尊和我們說話。

condescending (adj.) 居高臨下的

She spoke in a condescending tone.

她用居高臨下的語氣說話。

transcend (v.) 超越

Great art transcends time and culture.

偉大的藝術超越時間和文化。

offspring (n.) 後代

The cat's offspring were all healthy.

這隻貓的後代都很健康。

posterity (n.) 後代

We must protect the forest for posterity.

「我們必須為後代保護森林。」

condescend (v.) 屈尊

The manager condescended to listen to our ideas.

「經理屈尊聽取我們的想法。」

condescending (adj.) 居高臨下的

She spoke in a condescending tone.

「她用居高臨下的語氣說話。」

haughty (adj.) 傲慢的

The haughty princess looked down on everyone.

「傲慢的公主瞧不起每個人。」

arrogant (adj.) 自大的

His arrogant attitude made him unpopular.

「他自大的態度讓他不受歡迎。」

superior (adj.) 優越的

He felt superior to his classmates.

「他覺得自己比同學優越。」
