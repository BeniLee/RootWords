Part 1. 核心單字學習

bankrupt(adj.) 破產的

The company went bankrupt.

這家公司破產了。

erupt(v.) 爆發（火山）

The volcano erupted violently.

火山猛烈爆發。

outbreak(n.) 爆發、暴發

There was an outbreak of flu.

流感爆發了。

corrupt(adj.) 腐敗的

A corrupt officer was arrested.

一名腐敗官員被捕。

rupture(n./v.) 破裂、爆裂

The pipe suffered a rupture.

管路破裂了。

interrupt(v.) 打斷

Please don't interrupt me.

請不要打斷我。

disrupt(v.) 擾亂、中斷

The storm disrupted flights.

暴風天擾亂了航班。

abrupt(adj.) 突然的

The meeting ended abruptly.

會議突然結束。
