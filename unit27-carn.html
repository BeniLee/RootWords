Part 1. 核心單字學習

carnivore (n.) 肉食動物

Tigers are powerful carnivores.

老虎是強大的肉食動物。

carnivorous (adj.) 肉食的

The dinosaur was carnivorous.

這隻恐龍是肉食性的。

carnal (adj.) 肉體的

He gave in to his carnal desires.

他屈服於肉體的慾望。

carnation (n.) 康乃馨

He gave her a red carnation.

他送了她一朵紅色康乃馨。

incarnate (v./adj.) 化身

She is kindness incarnate.

她是仁慈的化身。

incarnation (n.) 化身

The actor was the perfect incarnation of the hero.

這位演員是那位英雄的完美化身。

reincarnation (n.) 輪迴

Some believe in reincarnation.

有些人相信輪迴。

carnival (n.) 嘉年華

The carnival is held every February.

嘉年華每年二月舉行。

discarnate (adj.) 無肉體的

Some believe in discarnate spirits.

「有些人相信無形體的靈魂。」
