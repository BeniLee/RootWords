Part 1. 核心單字學習

append (v.) 附加

Please append your signature at the end.

請在末尾附加你的簽名。

appendix (n.) 附錄、盲腸

The appendix contains extra information.

附錄包含額外的資訊。

appendicitis (n.) 闌尾炎

He was rushed to the hospital with appendicitis.

他因闌尾炎被緊急送醫。

pension (n.) 退休金

My grandfather lives on his pension.

我的祖父靠退休金生活。

pensioner (n.) 領退休金者

The park is popular among pensioners.

這個公園在領退休金者中很受歡迎。

compensate (v.) 補償

The company will compensate you for the loss.

公司會補償你的損失。

compensation (n.) 補償金

He received compensation for his injury.

他因受傷獲得了補償金。

reimburse (v.) 報銷

The company will reimburse your travel expenses.

公司會報銷你的旅費。

dispense (v.) 分配、配藥

The pharmacist dispensed the medicine.

藥劑師配發了藥物。

dispensary (n.) 藥房

The hospital has a 24-hour dispensary.

這家醫院有24小時藥房。
