Part 1. 核心單字學習

assist (v.) 幫助

Can you assist me with this heavy box?

你能幫我搬這個重箱子嗎？

resist (v.) 抵抗

I cannot resist the temptation of chocolate.

我無法抵抗巧克力的誘惑。

consist (v.) 由…組成

Water consists of hydrogen and oxygen.

水由氫和氧組成。

insist (v.) 堅持

He will insist on paying for dinner.

他堅持要付晚餐的錢。

persist (v.) 持續

If symptoms persist, see a doctor.

如果症狀持續，請看醫生。

exist (v.) 存在

Does life exist on other planets?

其他星球上有生命存在嗎？

consistent (adj.) 一致的

His story is consistent with the evidence.

他的說詞與證據一致。

persistent (adj.) 堅持不懈的

She is persistent in her efforts to learn.

她在學習上堅持不懈。

assistance (n.) 幫助

She needs financial assistance.

「她需要經濟援助。」

resistance (n.) 抵抗

The army put up strong resistance.

「軍隊進行了強烈的抵抗。」

irresistible (adj.) 無法抗拒的

The dessert was irresistible.

「這甜點讓人無法抗拒。」

insistence (n.) 堅持

At her insistence, we stayed for tea.

「在她的堅持下，我們留下來喝茶。」

perseverance (n.) 毅力

Hard work and perseverance lead to success.

「努力和毅力帶來成功。」

obstinate (adj.) 固執的

He is too obstinate to change his mind.

「他太固執了，不肯改變主意。」
