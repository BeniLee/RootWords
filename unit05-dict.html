Part 1. 核心單字學習

predict (v.) 預測

Can you predict the future?

你能預測未來嗎？

forecast (v./n.) 預報

The weather forecast says it will rain.

天氣預報說會下雨。

diction (n.) 措辭

Her diction is clear and precise.

她的措辭清晰精確。

dictionary (n.) 字典

Look up the word in the dictionary.

在字典裡查這個字。

benediction (n.) 祝福

The priest gave a benediction.

牧師給予祝福。

malediction (n.) 詛咒

The witch cast a malediction.

女巫下了詛咒。

dictum (n.) 名言

His dictum became famous.

他的名言變得很有名。

dictate (v.) 口述；命令

The boss likes to dictate everything.

老闆喜歡命令一切。

dictation (n.) 聽寫

We had dictation in class today.

今天課堂上我們做了聽寫。

dictator (n.) 獨裁者

The dictator ruled with an iron fist.

獨裁者以鐵腕統治。

contradict (v.) 反駁；矛盾

Don't contradict your own words.

不要自相矛盾。

contradiction (n.) 矛盾

There is a contradiction in his story.

他的說法有矛盾。

abdicate (v.) 退位；放棄

The king decided to abdicate the throne.

國王決定退位。
