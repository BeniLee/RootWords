Part 1. 核心單字學習

preside (v.) 主持

The judge will preside over the trial.

法官將主持審判。

president (n.) 總統

The president gave a speech.

總統發表了演講。

reside (v.) 居住

They reside in a small town.

他們居住在一個小鎮。

resident (n.) 居民

She is a resident of New York.

她是紐約的居民。

subside (v.) 消退

The flood waters began to subside.

洪水開始消退。

assiduous (adj.) 勤勉的

He is an assiduous student.

他是個勤勉的學生。

dissident (n.) 異議份子

The government arrested the dissident.

政府逮捕了異議份子。

sedate (adj.) 鎮靜的

The patient remained sedate.

病人保持鎮靜。

residence (n.) 住宅

They have a summer residence in the mountains.

「他們在山裡有棟夏季住宅。」

residential (adj.) 住宅的

This is a quiet residential area.

「這是個安靜的住宅區。」

diligent (adj.) 勤奮的

She is a diligent student.

「她是個勤奮的學生。」

industry (n.) 勤奮；工業

His success was due to hard work and industry.

「他的成功歸功於勤奮工作。」

insidious (adj.) 暗中為害的

The disease has an insidious onset.

「這種疾病有隱匿的發病過程。」

situation (n.) 位置；情況

The house is in a beautiful situation.

「這房子位於一個美麗的位置。」
