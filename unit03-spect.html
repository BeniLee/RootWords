Part 1. 核心單字學習

expect (v.) 預期；期待

I expect good results this time.

我這次期待好結果。

inspect (v.) 檢查；視察

The officer will inspect the building.

官員會檢查這棟建築。

inspector (n.) 檢查員

The inspector checked everything carefully.

檢查員仔細檢查了一切。

inspection (n.) 檢查；視察

The inspection will take two hours.

檢查需要兩小時。

circumspect (adj.) 謹慎的

Be circumspect when you make decisions.

做決定時要謹慎。

spectator (n.) 觀眾

The spectators cheered loudly.

觀眾們大聲歡呼。

spectacle (n.) 景象；奇觀

The fireworks were a beautiful spectacle.

煙火是一道美麗的景象。

spectacles (n.) 眼鏡

He wears spectacles for reading.

他戴眼鏡看書。

spectacular (adj.) 壯觀的

The view from the top was spectacular!

山頂的景色非常壯觀！

perspective (n.) 觀點；透視

Look at it from my perspective.

從我的角度來看。

prospective (adj.) 未來的；預期的

The prospective buyer looked at the house.

潛在買家看了這間房子。

prospect (n.) 前景；可能性

There is little prospect of improvement.

改善的前景渺茫。

suspect (v./n.) 懷疑；嫌疑犯

The police suspect the man.

警方懷疑那名男子。

respect (v./n.) 尊敬

You should respect your elders.

你應該尊敬長輩。

retrospective (adj.) 回顧的

The exhibition is a retrospective of her work.

這個展覽是她作品的回顧展。

aspect (n.) 方面；層面

Every aspect of the plan was considered.

計劃的各個方面都考慮到了。
