Part 1. 核心單字學習

event (n.) 事件

The concert was a major event.

這場音樂會是個重大事件。

invent (v.) 發明

He wants to invent a new machine.

他想發明一台新機器。

invention (n.) 發明

The telephone was a great invention.

電話是一項偉大的發明。

prevent (v.) 預防

Wash your hands to prevent disease.

洗手可以預防疾病。

adventure (n.) 冒險

They set out on an exciting adventure.

他們展開了一場刺激的冒險。

venture (n./v.) 冒險事業；冒險

The business venture was successful.

這項商業冒險成功了。

circumvent (v.) 規避

They tried to circumvent the rules.

他們試圖規避規則。

inventory (n.) 庫存

The store took inventory of its goods.

商店盤點了它的庫存。

coincidence (n.) 巧合

What a coincidence! I was there too.

「真巧！我也在那裡。」

creative (adj.) 有創意的

She has a very creative mind.

「她非常有創意。」

precaution (n.) 預防措施

Take precautions against the flu.

「採取預防流感措施。」

adventure (n.) 冒險

Life is an adventure.

「生活就是一場冒險。」

misfortune (n.) 不幸

They had the misfortune to lose their home.

「他們不幸失去了家園。」

mishap (n.) 小意外

The trip went well apart from a small mishap.

「除了個小意外，旅程很順利。」
