Part 1. 核心單字學習

lighten (v.) 照亮、減輕

The sun will lighten up the room.

陽光會照亮這個房間。

enlighten (v.) 啟發、教導

The teacher enlightened us about history.

老師啟發我們了解歷史。

enlightening (adj.) 有啟發性的

It was an enlightening experience.

這是一個有啟發性的經歷。

enlightenment (n.) 啟蒙

The Age of Enlightenment changed Europe.

啟蒙時代改變了歐洲。

delight (n./v.) 喜悅、使高興

The children were filled with delight.

孩子們充滿了喜悅。

delightful (adj.) 令人愉快的

We had a delightful picnic in the park.

我們在公園享受了一場愉快的野餐。

plight (n.) 困境

The charity helps people in plight.

這個慈善機構幫助困境中的人們。

kindle (v.) 點燃

She kindled a fire to keep warm.

她點燃了火堆來保暖。

radiate (v.) 發射、輻射

The sun radiates heat and light.

太陽散發熱和光。

radiation (n.) 輻射

Too much radiation can be harmful.

過多的輻射可能有害。
