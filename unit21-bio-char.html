Part 1. 核心單字學習

sloth (n.) 樹懶；懶惰

The sloth moves very slowly.

樹懶動作非常緩慢。

parasite (n.) 寄生蟲

The parasite lives on its host.

寄生蟲寄生在宿主身上。

incubate (v.) 孵化

The hen will incubate her eggs.

母雞會孵蛋。

assassinate (v.) 暗殺

They plotted to assassinate the leader.

他們陰謀暗殺領袖。

ambush (n./v.) 埋伏

The soldiers were caught in an ambush.

士兵們中了埋伏。

sluggish (adj.) 遲緩的

I feel sluggish after eating too much.

吃太多後我覺得沒精神。

leech (n.) 水蛭

A leech attaches itself to the skin.

水蛭附著在皮膚上。

cub (n.) 幼獸

The lion cub played with its mother.

獅子幼崽和媽媽玩耍。

slothful (adj.) 懶惰的

He is slothful and never exercises.

「他很懶惰，從不運動。」

parasitic (adj.) 寄生的

Some plants are parasitic.

「有些植物是寄生的。」

incubation (n.) 孵化期

The incubation period for the disease is two weeks.

「這種疾病的潛伏期是兩週。」

hatch (v.) 孵化

The eggs will hatch in three weeks.

「蛋將在三週後孵化。」

slug (n.) 蛞蝓

A slug left a trail on the ground.

「蛞蝓在地上留下一條痕跡。」

snail (n.) 蝸牛

The snail moved slowly across the path.

「蝸牛慢慢地爬過小徑。」
