Part 1. 核心單字學習

constitute (v.) 構成

Seven days constitute a week.

「七天構成一週。」

constitution (n.) 憲法

The constitution protects our rights.

「憲法保護我們的權利。」

constitutional (adj.) 憲法的

Freedom of speech is a constitutional right.

「言論自由是憲法賦予的權利。」

unconstitutional (adj.) 違憲的

The court ruled the law unconstitutional.

「法院判定該法律違憲。」

prostitute (n.) 妓女

She was forced to work as a prostitute.

「她被迫以賣淫為生。」

destitute (adj.) 貧困的

The destitute family had no money for food.

「這個貧困的家庭沒錢買食物。」

substitute (n./v.) 替代品；替代

Use honey as a substitute for sugar.

「用蜂蜜代替糖。」

constituent (n.) 選民

The senator met with his constituents.

「參議員會見了他的選民。」

consist of (phr.) 由…組成

Water consists of hydrogen and oxygen.

「水由氫和氧組成。」

compose (v.) 組成

The committee is composed of ten members.

「委員會由十名成員組成。」

constituency (n.) 選區

The MP serves her constituency well.

「這位議員為她的選區盡心服務。」

replacement (n.) 代替

He found a replacement for the broken part.

「他找到了破損零件的替代品。」

replace (v.) 取代

Robots will replace some workers.

「機器人將取代一些工人。」

substitution (n.) 替代

The coach made a substitution in the game.

「教練在比賽中進行了換人。」
