Part 1. 核心單字學習

perfect (adj.) 完美的

Practice makes perfect.

熟能生巧。

defect (n.) 缺陷

The product has a serious defect.

這個產品有嚴重的缺陷。

infect (v.) 感染

The virus can infect anyone.

這種病毒可以感染任何人。

infection (n.) 感染

Wash your hands to prevent infection.

洗手以防止感染。

affect (v.) 影響

The weather affects my mood.

天氣影響我的心情。

effect (n.) 效果

The medicine had an immediate effect.

這種藥產生了立即的效果。

effective (adj.) 有效的

This method is very effective.

這個方法非常有效。

disinfect (v.) 消毒

We need to disinfect the wound.

我們需要消毒傷口。

affection (n.) 喜愛

She has great affection for her pets.

她非常喜愛她的寵物。

contagious (adj.) 傳染的

The disease is highly contagious.

這種疾病具有高度傳染性。
