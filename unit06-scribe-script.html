單元06：【寫】-scribe- / -script-

Part 1. 核心單字學習

describe (v.) 描述

Can you describe what you saw?

你能描述你看到了什麼嗎？

description (n.) 描述

Her description was very detailed.

她的描述非常詳細。

prescribe (v.) 開藥方；規定

The doctor will prescribe some medicine.

醫生會開一些藥。

prescription (n.) 藥方；處方

Take this prescription to the pharmacy.

把這張處方籤拿到藥局。

subscribe (v.) 訂閱；贊助

I subscribe to that magazine.

我訂閱那本雜誌。

subscription (n.) 訂閱

My subscription expires next month.

我的訂閱下個月到期。

transcribe (v.) 抄寫；轉錄

Please transcribe the recording.

請抄寫這段錄音。

transcript (n.) 抄本；成績單

I need a transcript of my grades.

我需要一份成績單。

script (n.) 腳本；劇本

The actor studied his script.

演員研讀了他的劇本。

manuscript (n.) 手稿

She sent her manuscript to the publisher.

她把她的手稿寄給了出版社。

postscript (n.) 附言

She added a postscript at the end.

她在結尾加了一段附言。

circumscribe (v.) 限制；劃界線

The rules circumscribe our actions.

規則限制了我們的行動。

inscribe (v.) 銘刻

The ring is inscribed with her name.

戒指上刻有她的名字。

inscription (n.) 銘文；題字

The inscription is hard to read.

這段題字很難辨認。

scribble (v./n.) 潦草書寫

Don't scribble on your paper.

不要在紙上亂寫。
